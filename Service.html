“We design buildings and places that are purposeful, simple, efficient and beautiful.”

Capital Engineering Consultancy is one of the leading Consultancies in the U.AE. Over the past decade, we have worked on various projects ranging from the concept stage to detail drawings stage to authority submissions and construction stage.

We believe in Integrity and Commitment which are two important aspects in delivering excellent results to our clients.

At Capital Engineering Consultancy, Architectural Designs are unique in Concept and Bold in Execution.

Our scope of services for architecture include:

  1. 1. Concept Design Development
  2. 2. Detail Design Development
  3. 3. Preparation of Construction Documents and Drawings
  4. 4. Preparation of As Built Drawings
  5. 5. Feasibility Studies
  6. 6. Bidding and Negotiation
  7. 7. Construction and Contract Administration
  8. 8. Architect of Record
  9. 9. Project Management
